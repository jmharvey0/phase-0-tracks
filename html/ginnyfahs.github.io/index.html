This is a website all about banana art.

Many of us like bananas. Many of us like art.

So why aren't there more people talking about banana art?!?!?

Thankfully a small group of thoughtful, commmitted artists is changing the world by unleashing their creativity on bananas. Let us revel in their innovation and delight in their skill!

Bananas are the new portraiture.

I want to stay on the cutting edge of all banana art happenings moving forward.

One of the best ways to learn about banana art is to look to Japan. To quote the travel blog Tsunagu Japan, "Some Japanese people find the limited space and size of bananas to be a challenge and so carving expressions into them is a pastime that is getting more and more popular." As always, Japan is ahead of the curve! Not just in banana art--also in the beauty of their html websites :)
